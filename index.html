Passionate high-school STEM teacher dedicated to fostering critical thinking, scientific literacy, and inclusive learning through Universal Design for Learning.

My Teaching Approach

Thinking classrooms

I believe in the value of inquiry-based learning where students are able to practice not "what to think" but "how to think".

Scientific Literacy

Scientific literacy is one of the most important skills that students need to be equipped with when they leave highschool in order to navigate our increasingly technological and scientifically dependent world.

Collaborative Learning

Creating a supportive community where students learn from each other and develop teamwork skills.

Academic Resilience

Fostering academic resilience by creating an environment where learning and growth are always the goals, and students aren't afraid to make a mistake.

Real-World Context

Connecting classroom concepts to real-world applications and current scientific discoveries.

Scientific Inquiry

Guiding students through the process of asking questions, designing experiments, and drawing conclusions.
