Professional Development Plan

Year Planning

Goal: Complete year plans for Grade 11 & 12 Functions and Grade 10 Applied & Academic Science incorporating a thinking classroom style with scale-up and scale-down activities.

  • By Feb 3, 2025: Finish and test Grade 10 Academic Science plan; outline Grade 10 Applied using curriculum comparisons; review AT’s Grade 11–12 Functions plan.
  • Feb 3–Mar 1, 2025: Teach Grade 11 Functions, refine plan with AT guidance; pilot applied lessons in colleagues’ classrooms and gather feedback.
  • By Apr 29, 2025: Finalize all four course plans in a professional format with reflection spaces for ongoing journaling and improvement.

Scientific Literacy

Goal: Enhance ability to incorporate scientific literacy and communication skills into teaching practice.

  • By Feb 3, 2025: Brainstorm literacy-based assignments; audit past lessons for quick questioning activities; research resources with physics instructor.
  • Feb 3–Mar 1, 2025: Collaborate with AT and STEM colleagues to integrate literacy tasks; pilot a major assignment in Grade 11 Biology and collect peer feedback.
  • By Apr 29, 2025: Produce a polished literacy assignment and toolkit of mini-tasks to embed in any science lesson, plus reflection notes from initial implementations.

Assessment Strategies

Goal: Learn and apply varied assessment for/as/of learning techniques tailored to diverse course levels.

  • By Feb 3, 2025: Create exit tickets for past lessons; compile a list of formative/summative hybrid tasks (e.g., error-analysis quizzes, checkpoint quizzes tied to unit tests).
  • Feb 3–Mar 1, 2025: Refine and trial assessment ideas with AT and applied-course teachers; gather observations on student engagement and efficacy.
  • By Start of 2025–26: Develop a living assessment portfolio with templates and reflection logs for ongoing adaptation across course types.
